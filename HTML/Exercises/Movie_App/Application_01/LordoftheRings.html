The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring


Director: Peter Jackson


Summary: An epic account of the struggle between good and evil, the film chronicles the epic adventure of Frodo Baggins, a peaceful Hobbit who has to take on a tremendous responsibility. What Frodo must do is plunge into the realm of the Cracks of the Apocalypse to engage in an epic struggle and destroy this ring forever. Fortunately, he will not embark on this arduous journey alone: alongside Gandalf, Legolas, an elf, Gimli the dwarf; Aragorn the scout, the warrior Boromir, and Frodo's three friends, Merry, Pippin, and Samwise, who are also Hobbit like himself, come with him. This group advances through mountains, forests, snow, castles, rivers and plains, battling against the evil forces they encounter. By fulfilling the task of destroying the Ring, their goal is to erase the Dark Lords' reign from their reality.
